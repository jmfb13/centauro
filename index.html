Telling our history

Centauro, founded in 2017 in Austin, Texas, specializes in car transportation and has built a stellar reputation for providing top-tier services with a commitment to 100% customer satisfaction. Starting with a modest fleet, the company quickly expanded its local and interstate services by incorporating advanced logistics technology and focusing on personalized, reliable, and transparent service. Known for its safe and timely car deliveries, Centauro Transport actively engages with the Austin community, reinforcing its dedication to excellence and local support.
